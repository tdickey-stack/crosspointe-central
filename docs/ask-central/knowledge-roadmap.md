## Document 13 source plan

Document 13 will cover CrossPointe's Early Learning Center in greater depth. It will include the Mother's Day Out program, ages served, schedule, enrollment, tuition or fees, classroom expectations, contact process, closures, and other approved public information. Until that document is completed, Wayfinder should only provide the short approved description from Document 10 and direct detailed questions to the Early Learning Center or church office.

## Document 12 source plan

Document 12 will use the approved campus map plus staff-confirmed directions to describe:

- Buildings and entrances
- Rooms and ministry areas
- Welcome and check-in locations
- Accessible routes and entrances
- Parking and drop-off areas
- Common walking directions between campus destinations

The map image may be used as a source, but room names, accessibility details, and directions should be confirmed before publication. Wayfinder should give simple public directions without exposing private, staff-only, safety-sensitive, or restricted areas.

## Planned operational notices

Wayfinder needs a simple admin-managed notice layer for short-term changes such as:

- Care Center closed this week
- Temporary office or ministry closures
- One-time schedule or location changes
- Short-lived registration or availability notices

Each notice should include a topic, public message, effective date, expiration date, and active status. Active notices should temporarily outrank evergreen knowledge and automatically stop applying after expiration. This prevents temporary information from becoming permanent Firestore knowledge or requiring manual JSON edits.
