About CrossPointe Central

Your public digital hub for what is happening at CrossPointe Church.

Provided by: CrossPointe Church

CrossPointe Central helps visitors and church members find current church information, prepare for Sunday, connect with ministry opportunities, and keep useful resources together in one place.

What Central Does

CrossPointe Central brings together:

  • Current and upcoming church events.
  • Sunday sermon information and worship setlists.
  • Scripture reading and sermon-note tools.
  • Ministry campaigns, serving opportunities, and next steps.
  • Helpful CrossPointe resources and quick links.

Open to Everyone

Central is available to the public and does not require a CrossPointe Central account. Most features can be used without signing in to Google or any other service.

Optional Google Drive Integration

Central includes an optional Save to My Google Docs feature for sermon notes. When you choose this feature, Central asks you to sign in with Google and approve limited access to files created through Central.

Central uses this Google access only to create a new Google Doc in your own Google Drive and write the sermon notes you entered, along with relevant sermon details, into that document.

Central does not list, read, or change other files in your Google Drive. Google sign-in is optional, and choosing not to connect Google Drive does not prevent you from using Central's other features.

Your Information and Choices

Documents created with the Google Docs feature are stored in your Google Drive. CrossPointe Central does not store your Google password and does not sell Google user data or use it for advertising.

For more information, read the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
