Privacy Policy

How CrossPointe Central handles information and connected services.

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

CrossPointe Central is a digital resource provided by CrossPointe Church to help visitors and members access church information, events, resources, sermon content, and ministry tools.

Information We Collect

CrossPointe Central does not require users to create a CrossPointe Central account and does not intentionally collect or store personal information.

We do collect limited technical information through Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics to help us understand how the application is used and improve its performance. This information may include:

  • Device and browser information
  • Pages viewed
  • General usage statistics
  • Anonymous performance and diagnostic information

This information is used only to improve CrossPointe Central and is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Certain optional features connect directly to third-party services.

These may include:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Drive (for saving sermon notes)
  • YouVersion Bible (when available)

When you choose to use these features, authentication occurs directly with the respective provider. CrossPointe Central does not store your Google or YouVersion account credentials.

Any documents you choose to create or save are stored directly within your own Google Drive account, subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Likewise, any information associated with your YouVersion account is managed by YouVersion under their own policies.

Cookies

CrossPointe Central may use cookies or similar technologies that are required by Firebase, Google Analytics, Google authentication, or integrated third-party services. We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral marketing.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the integrity of CrossPointe Central. However, no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of CrossPointe Central constitutes acceptance of any revisions.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact CrossPointe Church.
